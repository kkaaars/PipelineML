Koko Collective was first discovered between two sisters with a passion for bringing you sustainable and ethically sourced items for your home. Each piece is considered and handmade, creating a unique item for every customer.
Brooke, Daniel and their little boy Nash took over KoKo collective in Sept 2024 after discovering their beautiful products when opening their Boutique children's store Little Palm Prints. Brooke was previously a Nurse and Daniel works hard behind the scenes in the Commercial world as a Builder.
Brooke and Daniel have always wanted to pursue another avenue in their careers and really look forward to bringing you all of KoKo collectives Designs whilst also bringing some fresh new designs to light.We will still be working closely together with our family of Indonesian crafters to ensure all our pieces are of the highest quality.
Our furniture is slowly made, meaning crafted in small batches by our family of artisans in Indonesia. There is sourcing, crafting, drying and finishing times that can not be rushed. As the rattan is a natural product there will also be colour and size variations to what you see in the products and that is what makes your products unique. Thankyou for stopping by.
Brooke, Daniel and Nash xxx
